10 ways to improve SEO:

  • 1.Google analytics

    Installing google analytics is a great tool for SEO as you can see various statistics about your website, using these statistics you can improve you SEO and website.

  • 2.Keyword optimization

    Researching and considering your audience and the phrases they use to locate your website is essential when selecting your keywords, picking the right keywords will boost your SEO significantly. You can use google tools to help with this process.

  • 3.Web page title

    One of the major considerations in your ranking a search engine will take into account is the title of your web page, selecting the correct web page title is very important and you should add keywords to your title making it easier for users to locate.

  • 4.URL optimization

    It is a common mistake to forget about your URL, when creating your URL you should include keywords and phrases. These will help with your websites ranking.

  • 5.Internal links

    Websites that reference other pages in the website with internal hyperlinks should be added, using theses hyperlinks, users will spend more time on your website as they can click through and use other parts of the website. The name of the hyperlink will also add quality to your website.

  • 6.ALT tags

    Alt tags should be used when adding images to your website, they should also be helpful to the user as good alt tags will help your websites ranking.

  • 7.Form Factor

    When designing your website, you must account for the fact that people will be using different types of devices with different view ports. Your website should be designed appropriately to accommodate for these different devices.

  • 8.Limit duplicate data

    Your website should limit having duplicate data, you should try to make your website unique as this will help your ranking and user retention.

  • 9.XML Sitemap

    Creating an XML sitemap and submitting the sitemap to google will help your ranking. This is so your website can be indexed.

  • 10.Error checker

    A broken link error checker should be implemented into your website to make sure if your website runs into any link errors you are notified using broken link checkers.
